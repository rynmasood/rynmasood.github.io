Data Deletion for Mappr

Mappr does not require a user account and does not store your saved places, history, or settings on external servers operated by Mappr.

Mappr stores app data locally on your Android device. This may include:

  • Saved places and favorites
  • Search history and route history
  • App settings and preferences
  • Last selected map location and related local state

How to Delete Your Mappr Data

You can permanently remove locally stored Mappr data in any of the following ways:

Option 1: Use the In-App Clear Data Setting

Open Mappr and go to:

Settings > Data Management > Clear All Data

This removes locally stored records such as saved places, history, route history, and app settings.

Option 2: Clear App Storage in Android Settings

On your Android device, go to:

Android Settings > Apps > Mappr > Storage > Clear Storage or Clear Data

You may also clear cache from the same screen, but clearing storage or app data is the step that removes locally stored records.

Option 3: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Mappr will remove the app and its locally stored data from your device, subject to normal Android behavior.

Important Note:
Mappr does not provide account-based cloud storage for saved places, history, or settings. Because there is no login account for these features, there is no separate “Delete Account” button.

If you use third-party services through the app, such as Google Play subscriptions, Google ads, analytics, crash reporting, or map and routing providers, those third parties may retain certain records under their own privacy policies.
